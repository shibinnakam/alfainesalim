About Alfaine

Welcome to a space dedicated to your health and prosperity. I am Alfaine Salim, a professional **Wellness Advisor** at IndusViva HealthSciences. This platform is designed to guide you through the intersection of ancient Ayurvedic wisdom and modern nutraceutical science.


IndusViva promotes Ayurveda-based nutraceuticals and a robust direct-selling business opportunity. Through our Community Commerce model, we advocate for natural health improvement and empower individuals through entrepreneurship. Our mission is to bring world-class wellness products to every household, established on January 17, 2014, at the World Trade Center, Bangalore.


As your advisor, I am committed to helping you achieve a balanced lifestyle through science-backed supplements and sustainable growth opportunities.
